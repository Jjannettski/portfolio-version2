About Me: My Story

I'm a Freelance Front-End Developer and Web Designer. My mission is to create sleek, functional, responsive websites/designs that achieve the ultimate goal of telling your story. I am available for hire.

I graduated from Baruch College with a Bachelor's in Corporate Communications with a minor in Psychology. From there, I entered the tech field when I began doing my graduate certification program at Rutgers University for Full-Stack Web Development.

I love exploring new cities, making new connections, forming lasting friendships, and trying new restaurants. In my spare time, I like to produce music (electronic) and play sports. I've played most sports you can think of, but hockey is my favorite. I'm also a die hard New York Ranger fan.

Now that i've told you my story, it's time to form yours.
